# Methods moves

Write Methods as an audit trail. Passive voice is acceptable when it keeps the procedure, cohort, or
measurement in focus.

## Establish cohort provenance {#methods-provenance}

- Decision: state design, centers, recruitment window, consecutive or randomized status, and source
  population in one early unit.
- Evidence: invariant across the corpus.
- Failure mode: eligibility criteria before the reader knows where or when the cohort came from.

## Report ethics precisely {#methods-ethics}

- Decision: name approval, consent disposition or waiver, and trial registration when applicable.
- Evidence: all families, with journal-specific placement.
- Failure mode: invented approval numbers or generic ethical-compliance language unsupported by source.

## Define eligibility clinically {#methods-eligibility}

- Decision: retain concrete thresholds, imaging criteria, time windows, treatment history, and exclusion
  logic exactly.
- Evidence: observational, imaging, and trial papers.
- Failure mode: compressing eligibility until the analyzed population cannot be reconstructed.

## Make endpoints reproducible {#methods-endpoints}

- Decision: define scale, threshold, adjudication time, assessor, blinding, and disagreement resolution.
- Evidence: especially important for diagnostic/imaging and neurovascular outcomes.
- Failure mode: endpoint labels without operational criteria.

## Give technical detail proportional weight {#methods-technical-detail}

- Decision: include acquisition, device, protocol, preprocessing, or procedure parameters needed for
  reproducibility; keep them subordinate elsewhere in the paper.
- Evidence: imaging papers carried the highest technical density and Methods passive ratio.
- Failure mode: deleting essential scanner or procedural parameters during language polishing.

## Explain analysis in execution order {#methods-statistics}

- Decision: state distributions and summaries, group tests, model construction, confounder handling,
  missing data, validation, sensitivity analyses, software, and alpha in a coherent order.
- Evidence: stable across families, with model-specific additions.
- Failure mode: a catalogue of tests disconnected from variables and endpoints.

## Preserve design-specific safeguards {#methods-safeguards}

- Decision: for trials retain randomization, concealment, blinding, estimand, analysis population, and
  multiplicity; for diagnostic work retain reference standard and reader independence.
- Failure mode: polishing away the features that justify the inference.
